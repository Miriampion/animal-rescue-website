There is something really special about emus

Emu are lovely and gentle animals naturally and given the chance will usually run away from a fight. They evolved into running machines that can maintain speeds of 30 mph for 30 minutes as they learned to run on their toes. This skill has resulted in very few natural predators. They travel in large groups when not breeding and this mob mentality works in their favour to discourage predation. When frightened, however, an emu has a dangerous double footed kick that farmers have learned to respect. Well maintained 6-foot fencing around large exercise pens satisfies their natural fence walking habits.
